### [2] [The quotient problem for linear recurrence sequences](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05784)
*Parvathi S Nair,S. S. Rout*

Main category: math.NT

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 经典丢番图问题关注线性递归序列比值U(n)/V(n)为整数的n的有限性。本文将其推广到二维情形(m,n)，允许存在一个增长缓慢（亚指数）的整数因子d_{m,n}，使得d_{m,n}U(m)/V(n)属于某个有限生成子环，旨在更一般框架下研究该问题的有限性。

Method: 采用Schmidt子空间定理，结合移动超平面、移动多项式和移动点等前沿工具，对序列结构进行精细分析，通过构造多项式P来分解序列中的奇异性，分别处理m≠n和m=n两种情形。

Result: 当m≠n时，存在多项式P使得d_{m,n}P(n)U(m)/V(n)为多重递归，且V(n)/P(n)为线性递归；当m=n时，d_{m,n}P(n)U(m)/V(n)和V(n)/P(n)均为线性递归。

Abstract: Let $\{U(m)\}_{m\in \N}$ and $\{V(n)\}_{n\in \N}$ be linear recurrence sequences. It is a well-known Diophantine problem to determine the finiteness of the set of natural numbers $n$ such that the ratio $U(n)/V(n)$ is an integer. We study the finiteness problem for the set $(m, n)\in \mathbb{N}^2$ such that there exist non-zero positive integers $d_{m, n}$ satisfying $\log |d_{m, n}|=o(n)$, and $d_{m, n}U(m)/V(n)$ is an element from a finitely generated subring of $\C$. To prove our results, we employ Schmidt's subspace theorem, and the concept of moving hyperplanes, moving polynomials, and moving points.
