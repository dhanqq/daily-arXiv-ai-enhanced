### [1] [Outer Type Severi-Brauer Schemes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.18983)
*Cameron Ruether*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文引入$\mathcal{O}$-模的降旗概念，定义了与一般线性群$\mathbf{GL}_{1,\mathcal{A}}$（Azumaya代数）的抛物子群层同构的理想旗层，并将其推广到外$A_n$型酉群情形。还定义了与抛物和Levi子群对同构的幂等元组层，展示了类型态射到Dynkin方案的表示，并推广了Severi-Brauer方案与Quillen构造。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为了在一般概形上统一描述Azumaya代数的抛物子群和酉群的结构，并建立旗层与群层之间的同构关系，从而为类型态射和Severi-Brauer方案提供新的代数几何框架。

Method: 通过定义$\mathcal{O}$-模的降旗概念构造理想旗层；将该概念扩展到外$A_n$型酉群情形；定义幂等元组层刻画抛物和Levi子群对；利用类型态射联系Dynkin方案；基于Severi-Brauer方案的纤维构造提出Quillen构造的新方法及其外版本。

Result: 成功构造了与抛物子群同构的旗层（包括一般线性群和酉群情形）；建立了类型态射到Dynkin方案的显式表示；给出了Severi-Brauer方案作为类型态射特定纤维的同构；提出了Quillen构造的新方法及其外版本（生成带酉对合的代数）。

Conclusion: 本文为Azumaya代数及其抛物子群提供了统一的旗层描述，并将Severi-Brauer方案与Quillen构造推广到外型情形，为相关代数几何结构的研究提供了新工具。

Abstract: We introduce the notion of a lowered flag of $\mathcal{O}$--modules in order to define a sheaf of flags of ideals isomorphic to the sheaf of parabolic subgroups for the general linear group $\mathbf{GL}_{1,\mathcal{A}}$ of an Azumaya algebra over a general scheme $S$. This notion is extended to the outer type $A_n$ case and we define a suitable sheaf of flags of ideals isomorphic to the sheaf of parabolic subgroups for a unitary group over $S$. When the group is suitably split these are related to flags of submodules in a vector bundle or in a vector bundle with hermitian form, respectively. We also define a sheaf of tuples of idempotents in the associated algebra which is isomorphic to the sheaf of parabolic and Levi subgroup pairs. We show how the type morphism from parabolic subgroups to the Dynkin scheme can be defined in terms of these sheaves of flags. We review how the Severi-Brauer scheme associated to an Azumaya algebra $\mathcal{A}$ is isomorphic to a particular fiber of this type morphism and we generalize this idea to the outer case in order to define outer Severi-Brauer schemes. We provide a new approach to Quillen's construction which produces an Azumaya algebra from a Severi-Brauer scheme and we show that an outer version of Quillen's construction also exists for outer Severi-Brauer schemes which produces an algebra with unitary involution.

</details>


### [2] [Weak and strong Lefschetz properties for Hartshorne-Rao modules of curves in $\mathbb P^3$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19434)
*Juan Migliore,Uwe Nagel,Chris Peterson,Ettore Teixeira Turatti*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文研究了射影空间$\mathbb P^3$中曲线的Hartshorne-Rao模$M(C)$是否满足弱与强Lefschetz性质，通过几何构造揭示了其与曲线几何配置的密切联系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探究曲线的几何结构如何影响其Hartshorne-Rao模是否具有Lefschetz性质（弱与强），填补该方向上的理论研究空白。

Method: 利用特殊化到零维方案（curvilinear schemes的并集）及一般希尔伯特函数结果；通过构造具体几何配置（如一般斜线、二次曲面上的曲线、光滑不可约曲线）进行正反例分析。

Result: （1）一般斜线并集：在$i=1,2,3$时乘法$L^i$具有最大秩；（2）二次曲面上的曲线及"所有线除一条外"在二次曲面上的斜线并集满足WLP；（3）对$r\geq10$，构造了"所有线除两条外"在二次曲面上的反例；（4）光滑不可约曲线在低次情况下满足WLP，但存在度15的反例，一般非退化有理曲线满足WLP。

Conclusion: 几何假设在控制Hartshorne-Rao模的Lefschetz性质中既有强大作用（如二次曲面情形），也存在局限性（如两条线偏离二次曲面即可破坏性质），为理解曲线模与代数性质的关系提供了新视角。

Abstract: Let $C\subset \mathbb P^3$ be a curve over an algebraically closed field of characteristic zero, and let $M(C)$ denote its Hartshorne-Rao module. We study how the geometry of $C$ influences whether $M(C)$ satisfies the Weak and Strong Lefschetz Properties. We first consider unions of general skew lines and prove that multiplication by $L^i$, for a general linear form $L$, has maximal rank on $M(C)$ for $i=1,2,3$. The proof uses a specialization to zero-dimensional schemes that can be written as a union of curvilinear schemes, each of a particular type and of degree at most three, together with generic Hilbert function results for such schemes, which are of independent interest. We then examine how special geometric configurations can affect the Weak Lefschetz Property. In particular, we show that curves on a smooth quadric surface have Hartshorne-Rao modules with the Weak Lefschetz Property, and that the property persists for unions of skew lines with all but one line on a quadric. By contrast, for $r\geq 10$, we construct configurations of $r$ skew lines with all but two lines on a quadric whose Hartshorne-Rao modules fail the Weak Lefschetz Property. Finally, we study smooth irreducible curves. We prove the Weak Lefschetz Property in several low-degree cases, construct a degree 15 curve for which it fails, and show that general nondegenerate rational curves have Hartshorne-Rao modules with the Weak Lefschetz Property. These results illustrate both the strength and the limitations of geometric hypotheses in controlling Lefschetz properties of Hartshorne-Rao modules.

</details>


### [3] [Bogomolov Decomposition and Compact K{ä}hler Manifolds of Algebraic Dimension Zero](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19713)
*Frederic Bruno Campana*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 该论文在条件性假设下证明：代数维数为零的紧Kähler流形本质上同构于Kummer流形与“简单”流形的乘积，后者猜想为双亚纯辛流形；并进一步证明四维“严格简单”流形只能是环面的平展商或全纯辛流形。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 试图分类代数维数为零的紧Kähler流形的结构，揭示其与Kummer流形、辛流形之间的关系。

Method: 利用条件性假设，结合代数几何与复几何方法，对流形的子簇结构进行分析和分类。

Result: (1) 代数维数为零的紧Kähler流形可分解为Kummer流形与“简单”流形的乘积；(2) 四维“严格简单”流形只能是环面平展商或全纯辛流形。

Conclusion: 该结果为理解低维复流形的分类提供了重要进展，并暗示了与辛几何的深层联系。

Abstract: We prove conditionally that compact K\''ahler manifolds of algebraic dimension zero are (essentially) isogeneous to products of Kummer and `simple' ones, the latter being conjecturally bimeromorphically symplectic. `Simple' means: its general point is not contained in a nontrivial subvariety. We also prove that four-dimensional `strictly simple' manifolds are either étale quotients of tori or holomorphically symplectic. `Strictly simple' means: its only subvarieties are points and itself.

### [4] [Motivic Galois theory for one-loop Feynman integrals in momentum space](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20106)
*Ulysse Mounoud*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文建立了一种基于动理学空间的 motivic 框架，用于处理单圈图费曼积分，该框架自然包含切割图，并研究了其函子性与权重分级结构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 已有的费曼表示框架无法自然处理带有切割的图，因此需要发展一种新的 motivic 框架，使切割图能够被统一纳入，并保持对图操作（边收缩和切割）的函子性。

Method: 为每个单圈图在一般运动学参数空间上关联一个 motivic 局部系统，并利用图上的边收缩和切割操作构造函子性映射，进而计算权重分级部分并推导 de Rham motivic Galois 群的（余）作用公式。

Result: 证明了该 motivic 局部系统的权重分级部分是 Tate 扭转的二次 Artin 动机，由最大切割商图给出；同时得到了 motivic Galois 群作用的显式表达式。

Conclusion: 该框架成功将切割图自然纳入 motivic 费曼积分理论，为理解费曼积分的代数结构和对称性提供了新工具。

Abstract: We develop a motivic framework for Feynman integrals of one-loop graphs in momentum space. Its advantage compared to the already existing framework in Feynman representation is that it naturally includes graphs with cuts. To each such graph, we associate a motivic local system over the space of generic kinematics. Our construction is functorial with respect to the natural operations on graphs: edge contraction and cutting. We compute the weight-graded pieces of the motivic local systems. They are Tate twists of quadratic Artin motives associated with maximally cut quotient graphs. We also derive a formula for the (co)action of the de Rham motivic Galois group, expressed in terms of cut quotient graphs.

</details>

### [5] [Fibonacci Numbers and Vieta Jumping for a Rational Diophantine Equation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19083)
*Steven J. Miller,Dimitrios Nikolakopoulos,Anitha Srinivasan*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 本文研究丢番图方程 (a+1)/b + (b+1)/a = k 的整数解，通过韦达跳跃法完全分类所有正整数对 (a,b)，证明 k 只能取 3 或 4，解与斐波那契数相关，且 (a+b)/gcd(a,b)² 仅取 1,2,3,5。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探究一个简单有理丢番图条件与韦达跳跃法及斐波那契数之间的意外联系。

Method: 使用韦达跳跃（Vieta jumping）方法对方程进行完整分类。

Result: k 只能为 3 或 4；解与经典斐波那契数相关；(a+b)/gcd(a,b)² 仅取 1,2,3,5。

Conclusion: 揭示了简单有理丢番图条件、韦达跳跃与斐波那契数之间的深层联系。

Abstract: We study the Diophantine equation $\displaystyle{\tfrac{a+1}{b} + \tfrac{b+1}{a} \ = \ k}$, where $k$ is an integer. Using Vieta jumping, we completely classify all positive integer pairs $(a, \, b)$. We prove that the associated integer value $k$ can only be $3$ or $4$. The corresponding solution pairs $(a,\,b)$ are related to the classical Fibonacci numbers. As a consequence, the quantity $\frac{a+b}{\gcd(a, \,b)^2}$ takes only the values $1, \, 2, \, 3$ and $5$. This reveals an unexpected connection between a simple rational Diophantine condition, Vieta jumping, and Fibonacci numbers.

</details>


### [6] [Halász theorems for Gaussian ideals in sectors and short intervals](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19105)
*Jan Kuś*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 本文证明了高斯整数环非零理想上乘法函数的定量Halász定理，包括扇区版本和扇区短区间版本。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将经典的Halász定理推广到高斯整数环的理想上，并研究其在扇区及短区间下的行为。

Method: 使用角傅里叶展开、范数压缩到自然数上的乘法函数，以及Mangerel定理。

Result: 获得了扇区短区间Halász定理，其中环状扇区的径向厚度趋于无穷。

Conclusion: 在非退化条件及角非假性条件下，扇区短区间和与无条件和的渐近比例一致。

Abstract: We prove a quantitative Halász theorem for multiplicative functions on the nonzero ideals of $\mathbb{Z}[i]$, with bounds controlled by pretentious distance to the Archimedean characters $N^{it}$. We also prove a sectorial analogue: under angular non-pretentiousness, the sum of $f$ over ideals lying in a fixed sector is asymptotically given by the expected proportion of the unrestricted sum. Finally, under angular non-pretentiousness and a non-degeneracy condition on conjugate prime pairs, we prove a sectorial short-interval version of the Halász theorem for annular sectors whose radial thickness tends to infinity. The proof of the sectorial short-interval Halász theorem uses angular Fourier expansion, norm-compression to multiplicative functions on $\mathbb{N}$, and a theorem of Mangerel.
