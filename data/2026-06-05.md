### [1] [Drinfeld modules in rank 2 with CM and S-unit j-invariants](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05820)
*Liam Baker,Fabien Pazuki,Patricio Perez Pina*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 本文证明了 $\mathbb{F}_q[T]$ 上秩2 Drinfeld 模的 $j$-不变量中，具有复乘法且属于 $S$-单位（$S$ 为偶数次素数的无限集）的集合是有限的。基于普通约化与超奇异约化的研究以及素数的分裂行为，给出了算法并计算了显式例子，包括 Dorman 猜想的反例。对于极大序 $\mathcal{O}$，通过泛性论证证明该算法计算了 Hilbert 模多项式 $H_\mathcal{O}$。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究 $\mathbb{F}_q[T]$ 上秩2 Drinfeld 模的 $j$-不变量的有限性，特别是那些具有复乘法且属于 $S$-单位的 $j$-不变量，并探讨 Dorman 猜想。

Method: 基于普通约化和超奇异约化的研究，以及素数分裂行为的分析；设计了以 $\mathbb{F}_q[T]$ 为系数、根为给定序的 CM $j$-不变量的多项式算法，并通过泛性论证将其用于计算 Hilbert 模多项式。

Result: 证明了 $\mathbb{F}_q[T]$ 上秩2 Drinfeld 模的 $j$-不变量中具有 CM 且为 $S$-单位的集合是有限的；给出了显式算法并计算了具体例子，找到了 Dorman 猜想的反例。

Conclusion: 该工作不仅建立了有限性结果，还提供了算法工具和显式反例，深化了对 Drinfeld 模 $j$-不变量和 Hilbert 模多项式的理解。

Abstract: We prove the finiteness of the set of $j$-invariants of Drinfeld modules of rank 2 over $\mathbb{F}_q[T]$ which are CM and $S$-units, for $S$ the infinite set of primes with even degrees. The proof is based on the study of ordinary reduction and supersingular reduction of Drinfeld modules, and on the splitting behaviour of primes dividing the difference of two Drinfeld singular moduli. We also provide an algorithm to compute a polynomial with coefficients in $\mathbb{F}_q[T]$ and roots the $j$-invariants having CM by a given order, and use it to compute some explicit examples, providing for instance counterexamples to a conjecture of Dorman. For a maximal order $\mathcal{O}$, we prove by a universality argument that our algorithm computes the Hilbert modular polynomial $H_\mathcal{O}$.

### [2] [A Generalization of the Amdeberhan-Andrews-Ballantine Conjecture](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06086)
*Rong Chen,Tianjian Xu*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 本文证明了一个关于双Lambert级数的Amdeberhan-Andrews-Ballantine猜想的推广形式，建立了系数由广义除数函数σ_k(n)给出的恒等式，其中原猜想是本文结果的特例。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 受Cui、Kumar和Singh关于该猜想是否存在推广形式的提问所驱动，旨在回答是否存在更一般的数学结构。

Method: 通过建立系数由广义除数函数σ_k(n)给出的恒等式，对原猜想进行推广和证明。

Result: 成功证明了一个广义恒等式，原猜想作为其中的特例被包含。

Conclusion: 确认了该猜想存在推广形式，并给出了一个更一般化的数学结果。

Abstract: In this paper, we prove a generalization of a conjecture of Amdeberhan, Andrews, and Ballantine on double Lambert series. Motivated by a question raised by Cui, Kumar, and Singh concerning the existence of a generalization of this conjecture, we establish an identity in which the coefficients are given by the generalized divisor function $σ_k(n)$. As a special case, our result includes the original conjecture.

### [3] [Coherent sheaves on subvarieties in Hopf manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06072)
*Liviu Ornea,Misha Verbitsky*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文证明了正规复解析簇X在具有可逆全纯收缩γ的条件下，具有仿射簇结构，并建立了γ-等变复解析自反凝聚层的代数化定理；进一步给出了X₀的结构定理以及商空间M到Hopf流形的嵌入，并证明了M上自反凝聚层的滤过性定理。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将经典的GAGA定理推广到带有全纯收缩的正常复解析簇上，建立解析对象与代数对象之间的对应关系，并研究相关商空间的结构性质。

Method: 利用全纯收缩γ构造仿射簇结构；通过C*作用研究X₀的几何；使用orbifold结构、Hopf流形嵌入和滤过序列等技术分析商空间M上的凝聚层。

Result: 1. X具有自然仿射簇结构，且γ-等变自反凝聚层可代数化；2. X₀具有C*自由作用，同构于Z上丰沛线丛的非零向量空间；3. M可嵌入Hopf流形，反之亦然；4. M上自反凝聚层存在特定滤过，其子商适当张量后为Z上层的拉回；5. M上自反凝聚层可滤过，伴随商秩≤1。

Conclusion: 建立了带有全纯收缩的复解析簇的GAGA型定理，揭示了此类簇及其商空间的深层代数结构，并为自反凝聚层提供了滤过性理论。

Abstract: We prove a version of GAGA theorem for a normal complex analytic variety $X$ equipped with an invertible holomorphic contraction $γ$ with center in $x$. We show that $X$ admits a natural structure of an affine variety, and any $γ$-equivariant complex analytic reflexive coherent sheaf on $X$ admits a natural algebraic structure. We prove a structure theorem for $X_0:=X\backslash x$, showing that it admits a proper action of ${\Bbb C}^*$, and is isomorphic to the space of non-zero vectors in the total space of an ample line bundle over the projective variety $Z:= X_0/{\mathbb C}^*$ equipped with an orbifold structure. We show that the quotient $M:=X_0/γ$ admits a holomorphic embedding to a Hopf manifold, and, conversely, any normal subvariety $M$ in a Hopf manifold is obtained this way. We prove a form of structure theorem, showing that any reflexive coherent sheaf on $M$, $\dim M > 2$, admits a filtration such that its associated graded subquotients, tensored with an appropriate line bundle, are obtained as pullbacks of coherent sheaves on the projective variety $Z=X_0/{\mathbb C}^*$. This is used to show that any reflexive coherent sheaf on $M$ is filtrable, that is, admits a filtration with associated graded quotients of rank $\leq 1$.

</details>


### [4] [Higgs bundles on the Fargues-Fontaine curve](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06092)
*Ho Leung Fong*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文在Fargues-Fontaine曲线上引入了Higgs丛的概念，建立了BNR对应，研究了Picard栈的作用，并证明了与Hitchin纤维的等价关系，最后讨论了与数论对象的联系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Higgs丛理论推广到Fargues-Fontaine曲线这一算术几何对象上，并建立其与数论对象（如Springer纤维）之间的深层联系。

Method: 通过定义Fargues-Fontaine曲线上的Higgs丛，建立BNR对应（将Higgs丛与合适曲线上的线丛联系起来），描述Picard栈的作用，并证明从$B_{dR}^+$-仿射Springer纤维的乘积到Hitchin纤维的自然单射诱导几何点上的范畴等价。

Result: 建立了BNR对应；证明了存在从$B_{dR}^+$-仿射Springer纤维乘积到Hitchin纤维的自然单射，该映射在几何点上诱导范畴等价。

Conclusion: 本文的工作将Higgs丛理论与Fargues-Fontaine曲线结合，建立了与数论对象的联系，为进一步研究算术几何中的对应关系提供了新工具。

Abstract: In this paper, we introduce a notion of Higgs bundles on the Fargues-Fontaine curve. We establish a version of the BNR correspondence, which relates Higgs bundles to line bundles on suitable curves. We then describe an action of a Picard stack on the moduli stack of Higgs bundles and show that, modulo this action, there is a natural injective map of étale-stacks from the product of $B_{dR}^+$-affine Springer fibers to the Hitchin fiber that induces an equivalence of categories on every geometric point. Finally, we discuss connections with number-theoretic objects.

</details>
