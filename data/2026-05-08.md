### [1] [On the cohomological purity of the affine Springer fibers](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05596)
*Zongbin Chen*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文研究了仿射Springer纤维的上同调纯性问题，证明了仿射Springer纤维上同调纯当且仅当其ξ-稳定商上同调纯，并等价于某截断序列的上同调纯，进而给出了层论重述和微局部分析比较，最后证明其原始部分上同调和ξ-稳定商上同调仅依赖于定义元素的根赋值数据。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 回应Gérard Laumon和Jean-Loup Waldspurger关于仿射Springer纤维上同调纯性的问题。

Method: 通过建立仿射Springer纤维、ξ-稳定商与截断仿射Springer纤维之间的上同调纯性等价关系，结合层论重述和交上同调复形的微局部分析进行比较。

Result: 仿射Springer纤维上同调纯当且仅当其ξ-稳定商上同调纯，且等价于某截断序列上同调纯；原始部分上同调和ξ-稳定商上同调仅依赖于根赋值数据。

Conclusion: 给出了仿射Springer纤维上同调纯性的新判据，并揭示了其与根赋值数据的本质依赖关系。

Abstract: We address questions posed by Gérard Laumon and Jean-Loup Waldspurger concerning the cohomological purity of affine Springer fibers. More precisely, we show that an affine Springer fiber is cohomologically pure if and only if its $ξ$-stable quotient is cohomologically pure, and that this is further equivalent to the cohomological purity of a certain sequence of truncated affine Springer fibers. We deduce from this a sheaf-theoretic reformulation of cohomological purity for affine Springer fibers. We then compare this new criterion with a previously known one via a microlocal analysis of the relevant intersection complexes. As a corollary, we show that both the primitive part of the cohomology of an affine Springer fiber and the cohomology of its $ξ$-stable quotient depend only on the root valuation datum of the defining element.

### [3] [Geography and Deformations of $\mathbb{Z}_2^s$-Covers of General Type Over Weighted Projective Threefolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05665)
*Patricio Gallardo,Jayan Mukherjee*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文研究了作为加权射影空间$\mathbb{Z}_2^s$-覆盖的一般型三维流形，重点关注其不变量、形变理论以及$m$-典范映射的行为，得到了体积与拓扑和全纯欧拉示性数比值的渐近行为与界，给出了Hunt猜想的反例，推广了覆盖在模空间中一般性的判别准则，并利用有限群上的傅里叶变换完全分类了$\mathbb{Z}_2^s$-覆盖何时为平坦多典范映射。
