### [1] [Planes in quadratic 4-space and associated shapes of lattices](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.03472)
*Menny Aka,Konstantin Andritsch,Andreas Wieser*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 本文研究了四维二次空间中非退化有理平面与Bianchi轨道流形上周期测地线之间的自然联系，并在Linnik型分裂条件下证明了这些对象的同步等分布性质，主要依赖于Einsiedler和Lindenstrauss的高秩对角化作用连接的分类结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立四维二次空间中有理平面、Bianchi轨道流形上的周期测地线、模曲线上的CM点与周期测地线之间的自然耦合关系，并研究它们在Linnik型分裂条件下的同步等分布性质。

Method: 利用Einsiedler和Lindenstrauss关于齐次空间上高秩对角化作用连接的分类理论作为主要工具。

Result: 证明了在Linnik型分裂条件下，上述所有几何对象能够实现同步等分布。

Conclusion: 该工作揭示了不同几何对象之间的深层耦合关系，并为更广泛的等分布问题提供了新的技术途径。

Abstract: Let $Q=-x_1^1-x_2^2-x_3^2+x_4^2$ be the standard signature $(1,3)$ quadratic form. To each non-degenerate rational plane $L$ in the four-dimensional quadratic space $(\mathbb{Q}^4,Q)$ we can naturally attach a periodic geodesic on the Bianchi orbifold $\mathrm{SL}_2(\mathbb{Z}[i])\backslash \mathbb{H}^3$ which records the position of $L$ in the Grassmannian up to integer rotations. Moreover, each such plane $L$ defines a CM point and a periodic geodesic on the modular curve through restriction of $Q$ to $L$ and its orthogonal complement. Lastly, the local isomorphism between $\mathrm{SO}_{1,3}(\mathbb{R})$ and $\mathrm{SL}_2(\mathbb{C})$ gives rise to a further periodic geodesic on the Bianchi orbifold.
  In this article, we exhibit a natural coupling of all the above objects and prove simultaneous equidistribution under a Linnik-type splitting condition. The main ingredient is the classification of joinings of higher-rank diagonalizable actions on homogeneous spaces due to Einsiedler and Lindenstrauss.

### [2] [Syzygies of Isotropic Kalman Varieties](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02921)
*Suhas Vadan Gondi,Sarah Kumar,Abhik Pal*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文在辛向量空间上推广了A型Kalman簇，定义了C型Kalman簇（同构子空间不变性），并研究了其定义方程、几何不变量和奇异性，同时推测了长正合列的存在性，最后推广到B型和D型正交情形。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将经典的A型Kalman簇（复向量空间中具有不变子空间的态射）推广到辛向量空间（C型）和正交向量空间（B型和D型），研究更广泛李型下的不变子空间问题。

Method: 在辛向量空间中，固定迷向子空间W满足W^⊥ = W ⊕ L，定义C型Kalman簇为辛态射中具有指定维数余迷向不变子空间的集合；通过类比A型结果，推导定义方程、几何不变量，并分析奇点结构。

Result: 建立了C型Kalman簇与A型Kalman簇的类比结果，包括定义方程、几何不变量和奇异性分析；推测了结构层的长正合列存在性；并进一步描述了B型和D型正交情形的对应构造。

Conclusion: 本文成功将Kalman簇从一般线性群推广到辛群和正交群，为理解不同李型下不变子空间簇的几何性质提供了统一框架，并提出了值得进一步研究的长正合列猜想。

Abstract: Let $L$ be a subspace of a complex vector space $V$ and fix $s \leq \dim{L}$. The (type A) Kalman variety consists of all endomorphisms of $V$ that have an $s$-dimensional invariant subspace in $L$. We introduce a generalization where $V$ and $L$ are symplectic vector spaces. We fix an isotropic subspace $W \subseteq V$ satisfying $W^\perp = W \oplus L$. The isotropic (type C) Kalman variety consists of symplectic morphisms of $V$ that have an invariant coisotropic subspace of a prescribed dimension inside $W^\perp$. We are mainly interested in studying the Lagrangian case. In type C, we prove analogues of results known for type A Kalman varieties; in particular, we determine the defining equations, compute geometric invariants, and analyze their singularities. We conjecture the existence of a long exact sequence relating the structure sheaves. Based on the results in the symplectic case, we describe Kalman variety analogues with respect to endomorphisms of odd orthogonal (type B) and even orthogonal (type D) vector spaces.

</details>


### [3] [Bondal's conjecture in dimension five](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.03424)
*Stéphane Druel,Jorge Vitório Pereira,Brent Pym,Frédéric Touzet*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文首次证明了Bondal猜想在五维Fano流形上成立，并给出了所有奇数维Fano流形的部分结果。证明综合运用了三个关键技术：弱Fano流形上余一维叶状结构的代数可积性准则、Poisson结构的模残差方法，以及Pfaff场的不变子簇的上同调约束。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Bondal猜想为Poisson Fano流形的退化轨迹（Poisson结构秩下降的位置）提供下界，此前仅知对于不超过四维的Fano流形成立，本文旨在将该猜想推广到更高维度。

Method: 证明方法包括：(i) 将第一作者先前关于弱Fano流形上余一维叶状结构的代数可积性准则进行推广；(ii) 利用Gualtieri和第三作者引入的Poisson结构的"模残差"；(iii) 将Esteves-Kleiman的结论推广到子簇上Pfaff分布在允许闭强定向正电流情形下的上同调约束。

Result: 首次证明了Bondal猜想对五维Fano流形成立，并给出了所有奇数维Fano流形的部分结果。

Conclusion: 本文推进了Bondal猜想在Poisson几何中的研究，将已知验证维度从四维提升至五维，并为更高奇维度的情形提供了部分进展。

Abstract: Bondal's conjecture in Poisson geometry gives lower bounds on the degeneracy loci of Poisson Fano manifolds, where the rank of the Poisson structure drops. By work of several authors, it was previously known to hold for Fano manifolds of dimension at most four. We give the first proof of this conjecture for Fano manifolds of dimension five, and partial results for Fano manifolds of all odd dimensions. The proof uses: (i) an algebraic integrability criterion for codimension-one foliations on weak Fano manifolds, extending a previous result of the first author; (ii) the "modular residues" of Poisson structures introduced by Gualtieri and the third author; and (iii) a cohomological constraint on invariant subvarieties for Pfaff fields, extending earlier results of Esteves--Kleiman to the case in which the Pfaff distribution on the subvariety admits a closed strongly directed positive current.

</details>
